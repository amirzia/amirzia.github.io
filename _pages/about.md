I am a forth-year bachelor student in Sharif University of Technology majored in Computer Engineering. My main research interests are:

- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- Network Science
- Complexity
- Time series forecasting

Currently I am working on a project aiming to collect quality Internet network traffics and conduct Web Fingerprinting Attacks (WFA) on them using Deep Learning techniques. This project is supervised by [Mahdi Jafari](http://sharif.edu/~mjafari). Another project I am working on is about inferring missing links and cascades in a partially observed network, under supervision of [Hamidreza Rabee](http://sina.sharif.ac.ir/~rabiee/). Prior to these, I collaborated with [Sasan Barak](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=C53gU8gAAAAJ&hl=en) on a project related to Time Series Forecasting.

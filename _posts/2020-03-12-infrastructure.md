As of writing this, March 2020, the whole world is facing a global pandemic. A complicated virus known as COVID-19 has spread to all countries. This virus is very contagious. Infected individuals may transmit the virus pretty quickly in public and crowded places. Because of this, most people are in home-quarantine since several weeks ago. It seems that this situation will continue for a while, as the number of infected people is growing fast and it has not reached its peak.

During this period, the availability of proper infrastructure is of great importance. Without accessibility to electricity, gas, and clean water this quarantine, on such scale, would become very difficult. Thanks to this infrastructure, many people are able to stay at their home for months. That was not possible a few hundred years ago. It was not possible a few hundred years ago.

With the advancement of technology, another key infrastructure plays a vital role. The Internet. Many take advantage of this fascinating invention as a tool for entertainment, learning, and communication. Besides, a lot of companies made the majority of the employers work remotely at their home.

I hope scientists find a cure for this disease soon and our life return to the normal state.
